Our Commitment to Preventing Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Last Updated: 06-04-2025

At Shortart Software private limited, we are firmly committed to protecting children and preventing any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. We have a zero-tolerance policy towards any content or behavior that involves abuse, endangerment, or exploitation of children.

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexualized content involving minors
  • Grooming or attempts to engage minors in inappropriate interactions
  • Harmful or exploitative content involving children

Such content is strictly forbidden and will be immediately removed, reported to the appropriate authorities, and result in a permanent ban of the involved user(s).

2. Proactive Monitoring

We use automated tools, moderation systems, and human review to detect and remove CSAE content, including:

  • Blocking known CSAM using tools like PhotoDNA and hash-matching technologies
  • Flagging suspicious behavior or content for manual review

3. Reporting Tools for Users

Users can report content or users they believe may be involved in CSAE through our in-app tools or by contacting us via email. We treat all reports seriously and act promptly.

4. Enforcement and User Actions

  • Immediate removal of CSAE content
  • Suspension or permanent banning of accounts
  • Collaboration with legal authorities when applicable

5. Cooperation With Law Enforcement

We fully cooperate with law enforcement and child protection agencies. When necessary, we share relevant user data, reports, and evidence to assist in investigations and prosecutions, in accordance with the law.

6. Educational Efforts

We are committed to ongoing training for our team and educational outreach to inform users about safety and CSAE prevention. We also partner with child protection organizations when possible.
